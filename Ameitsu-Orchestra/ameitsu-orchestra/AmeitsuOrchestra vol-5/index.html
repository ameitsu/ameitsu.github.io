这个是老师留的作业，要求是写一个和春节有关系的文段。看过了学长的迷之示例（2100 年春节他们家去莫斯科玩，不小心引起了火灾，最后还被活了一百多年的普京提溜走了。普京人还怪好的，给他们做饺子，结果吃饺子的时候普京自己噎④了）后我们组也要整一个！

It was Spring Festival of year 2100, I got together with my family and went to Mars for the special holiday. We highly valued this trip, as my family can reunite on such a marvelous world. Our spaceship was super cutting-edge, therefore we arrived in less than a day. After we landed on mars, we immediately realized that we had landed at the incorrect Mars station, which was a miserable thing.

Luckily, a group of Chinese scientists brought us back. They showed their hospitality by giving us some Mars delicacy —— Mars fish. As we know, fish carries unique meanings in Chinese culture, and it was amazing to see that Chinese people still keep and practise the original customs. While we were cooking the fish to make dumplings, we noticed the smell of iron, but we didn’t pay attention. In the end, we found that the fish was full of iron (III) oxide, a common mineral on Mars, but it was too late as we had finished it.

Fortunately, a guy called Elon Musk, who owned SpaceX, decided to aid us. He provided us some basic medicine and an even better reunion dinner! To get us fully recovered, Elon lent us his Starship to send us back home, but it exploded again and lit up the Martian night sky. Nevertheless, we took the escape pod and evacuated in time. A Chinese spaceship found us and we boarded it. We talked about the old times and shared views about Mars tourism with the crew on the way back. What an exhilarating Spring Festival!
